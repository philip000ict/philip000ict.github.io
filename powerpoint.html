Grade 7 PowerPoint Games

    Instructions

  • Divide class into 2-3 groups
  • Each group takes turns to choose 2 tiles
  • Click on the two tiles and they will turn over to reveal a word or picture
  • If the word matches the picture they get a point
  • If the tiles do not match, click on them to turn them face down again
  • Next group takes a turn until all tiles have been matched
