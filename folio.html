Databack Form Server

In 2015 a team from Deakin University put together an app for estimating the location of unexploded ordnance in the beautiful but endangered country of Laos. Using freely available flight mission records the GIS coordinates were plotted onto a GoogleMap to enable assessment of UXB probable location.

To find recorded events a click on a location in the left panel would bring up a detailed map with the events plotted as markers. To click upon the marker opens a panel with a picture of ordinance to be aware of and most importantly the likelihood that the Event resulted in a UXB, or environmental contamination

If the result indicates that the device was activated, then the UXB likelihood was low, but if there was no observed result, then the probability of UXB contamination remains high
